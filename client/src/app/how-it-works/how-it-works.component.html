How it works

1. Login with your existing Google or Apple account

If you are not yet a Dish Truck member, after logging in you will have a chance to create a Dish Truck account. Your account is linked to the payment option of your choice. The cost is $1 per set of dishes per use. Dishes must be returned within 30 days, or a replacement fee of $10 - $20 per set will be charged.

2. Get a set of dishes

We are currently participating with GreenStar Co-Op and various food vendors at GrassRoots Fest and more to come.

3. Confirm and check out

You will receive a confirmation screen to show the vendor proof of purchase.

4. Return your dish

Find a dish return station in this app. Then scan the QR Code at the return site to return your dishes.

5. Feel the gratitude that comes with Bringing Sustainability to the Party!

Together with folks like you, Dish Truck as helped keep over 10,000 pieces of garbage and 60 gallons of compost out of the landfill! Side effects include a sense of relief, problem solving and standing ovations. Thank You! Gracias!
