How it works

1. Login with your existing Google or Apple account

If you are not yet a Dish Truck member, after logging in you will have a chance to create a Dish Truck account. The cost is $1 per set of dishes per use. Dishes must be returned within 30 days, or a replacement fee of $10 - $20 per set will be charged. You will be billed at the end of the month

2. Get a set of dishes

We are currently participating with GreenStar Food Co-Op with more to come.

3. Confirm and check out

You will receive a confirmation screen to show the vendor proof of purchase.

4. Return your dish

Find a dish return station using the Return Dish . Then either (1) scan the QR Code at the return site OR (2) use the Return Dish menu item and click the return site you are using.

5. Pay bill at the end of each month

You will receive a monthly bill, charging $1 per use. You may pay with Google Pay, Apple Pay, Paypal or a credit card.

6. Feel the gratitude that comes with Bringing Sustainability to the Party!

Together with folks like you, Dish Truck as helped keep over 10,000 pieces of garbage and 60 gallons of compost out of the landfill! Side effects include a sense of relief, problem solving and standing ovations. Thank You! Gracias!
