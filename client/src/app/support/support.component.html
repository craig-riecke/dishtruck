FAQ

How does payment work?

You will not see an immediate charge on your account. Accrued charges for dish rentals will be charged on the last day of every month.

*Replacement fees for any dishes not returned within 30 days will be charged after the 30th day: $10 for plastic clamshells, $20 for metal containers.
